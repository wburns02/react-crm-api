Septage Disposal Facilities & Dump Stations
INTERNAL REFERENCE — Central Texas (I-35 Corridor)
TCEQ Compliance Reminder All septage haulers must comply with 30 TAC Chapter 312 (Sludge Use, Disposal, and Transportation). Every load requires a manifest/trip ticket showing origin, volume, waste type, and destination. All vehicles must carry a valid TCEQ transporter registration. Domestic septage only at most municipal receiving stations — grease trap and industrial waste typically require separate disposal.

How to Find Additional Disposal Sites
  • TCEQ Central Registry: Search for Chapter 312 (Sludge) permits in your target counties to find private land application sites not listed here. tceq.texas.gov/crpub
  • TCEQ Sludge Program: tceq.texas.gov/permitting/registration/sludge
  • Texas On-Site Wastewater Association (TOWA): Member directory and disposal facility resources. txowa.org
  • TCEQ Transporter/Hauler FAQ: tceq.texas.gov (Transporters FAQ)
  • Ask fellow haulers: The septic community is tight-knit. Other haulers often share dump site info.
