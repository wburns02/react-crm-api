An optional add-on that builds up service credit each month so you're not caught off guard when something breaks. $20/month accrues as credit toward future repairs, parts, and pump-outs -- whatever your system needs. ========================================
1. HOW IT WORKS
========================================

$20/month accrues as Repair Credit on your account.

- Billing is added to your existing monthly charge ($29 maintenance + $20 credit = $49/month total)
- Credit accrues each month and rolls over -- it never expires while your maintenance plan is active
- Credit is automatically applied to invoices for any non-covered service
- You can check your credit balance any time by calling 512.737.8711


========================================
2. WHAT YOUR CREDIT CAN BE APPLIED TO
========================================

- Pump-outs (sludge removal, tank pumping)
- Parts replacement (aerators, pumps, floats, timers, control panels, spray heads)
- System repairs and adjustments outside of routine maintenance
- Emergency service calls
- Any other invoiced MAC Septic service

NOTE: The credit does not pre-pay for any specific service or guarantee a fixed price. It's a balance you've built up that gets applied to whatever your invoice happens to be.


========================================
EXAMPLE
========================================

You sign up for the Repair Credit Plan in January. You pay $20/month for 18 months -- that's $360 in credit. In July of the following year, your aerator fails and the repair invoice is $480. Your $360 credit is automatically applied. You owe $120 instead of the full $480.


========================================
3. NON-REFUNDABLE IN CASH
========================================

Your accrued credit is non-refundable in cash. It can only be applied to MAC Septic services. This is not a deposit, escrow account, or savings account -- it is prepaid service credit.


========================================
4. CANCELLATION & USE OF REMAINING CREDIT
========================================

You may cancel the Repair Credit Plan at any time with 30 days' written notice. Cancellation of the Repair Credit Plan does not cancel your underlying maintenance agreement.

Upon cancellation:
- Monthly credit billing stops at the end of the 30-day notice period
- Any remaining credit balance must be used within 90 days of cancellation
- Unused credit after 90 days is forfeited and cannot be refunded or recovered

If your underlying maintenance agreement is cancelled, the Repair Credit Plan is automatically cancelled as well, and the same 90-day window applies.


========================================
5. TRANSFER OF CREDIT
========================================

Credit balances are tied to the property listed above and may be transferred with the sale of the property if the new owner signs a maintenance agreement with MAC Septic within 30 days of closing. Otherwise, credit balances are non-transferable.


========================================
6. NATURE OF THIS AGREEMENT
========================================

This Repair Credit Plan is a prepayment for future services to be rendered by MAC Septic Services. It is not:

- A deposit, escrow account, or trust account
- A warranty or service contract guaranteeing repair coverage at any specific dollar amount
- A financial product, savings vehicle, or insurance policy

MAC Septic is not a financial institution and does not hold customer funds in trust. All credit is recognized as prepaid revenue and applied to invoices as services are rendered.
