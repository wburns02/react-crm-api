1. SCOPE OF SERVICE
========================================

MAC Septic Services ("Provider") agrees to provide three (3) maintenance visits per year to the property listed above. Each visit includes:

- Inspection of all treatment compartments (trash tank, aerobic chamber, pump tank, chlorination)
- Testing of disinfection system (chlorine residual or UV output)
- Inspection and testing of aerator, pumps, floats, and alarms
- Inspection of spray heads, drip lines, or other distribution components
- Sludge level measurement
- Minor adjustments, cleaning, and chlorine tablet replenishment
- Filing of maintenance report with the applicable county authority

Visits are scheduled approximately every four (4) months. Provider will contact Customer at least seven (7) days before each visit to confirm scheduling.

========================================
3. EXCLUSIONS
========================================

This agreement covers routine maintenance only. The following are NOT included and will be quoted separately:

- Pump-outs (sludge removal / tank pumping)
- Parts replacement (aerators, pumps, floats, timers, control panels)
- Repairs to spray heads, drip lines, or distribution systems
- Emergency service calls outside of scheduled visits
- System modifications, upgrades, or replacements
- Damage caused by third parties, flooding, power surges, or acts of nature

Provider will notify Customer of any recommended repairs discovered during a maintenance visit and provide a written estimate before performing additional work.


========================================
4. TERM & RENEWAL
========================================

This agreement is effective on the date signed below and continues for an initial term of twelve (12) months.

This agreement automatically renews for successive 12-month terms unless either party provides written notice of cancellation at least thirty (30) days before the renewal date. Written notice may be delivered by email, US mail, or phone call to the Provider.

Customer may cancel at any time with 30 days' notice. If Customer cancels mid-term, monthly billing stops at the end of the 30-day notice period. There is no cancellation fee and no refund for the current billing cycle.


========================================
5. TCEQ COMPLIANCE & COUNTY REPORTING
========================================

Provider is a TCEQ-licensed maintenance provider and will maintain this contract on file as required by 30 Texas Administrative Code, Chapter 285.

Customer authorizes Provider to:

- File maintenance reports with the applicable county environmental health office after each visit
- Report system status, test results, and any deficiencies to the county as required by local ordinance
- Act as the maintenance provider of record for the property's OSSF permit

If Customer has an existing maintenance contract with another provider, Customer is responsible for terminating that agreement. Only one provider may be the maintenance provider of record for a given OSSF permit.
