Why the Pump-Out Matters

The tank is supposed to have water and effluent in it — that's normal.

The problem is the scum on top and solids on the bottom. When those build up, they push out into the pump and the drain field.

A pump replacement is $3K–$5K. A field replacement is $5K–$7K. The $625 pump-out removes the exact stuff that destroys both.
